Dataset Description#

In this dataset, there are 22 columns in total: 7 numeric columns, 14 binary columns (dummy), and the dependent column of interest (Diabetes_012).

‘Diabetes_012’: indicates that 0 is for no diabetes or only during pregnancy, 1 is for prediabetes, and 2 is for diabetes.
‘HighBP’: indicates that 0 is for low blood pressure, 1 is for high blood pressure
‘HighChol’: indicates that 0 is for low cholesterol level, 1 is for high cholesterol level
‘CholCheck’: indicates that 0 is for no cholesterol check within 5 years, 1 is for cholesterol check within 5 years
‘BMI’: Body mass index level
‘Smoker’: indicates that 0 is for never had more than 100 cigarettes, 1 is for had more than 100 cigarettes
‘Stroke’: indicates that 0 is for never had a stroke, 1 is for had a stroke
‘HeartDiseaseorAttack’: indicates that 0 is for not having coronary heart disease, 1 is for having coronary heart disease
‘PhysActivity’: indicates that 0 is for no physical activity in last 30 days, 1 is for had done physical activity in last 30 days
‘Fruits’: indicates that 0 is not consuming at least 1 fruit/day, 1 is for consuming at least 1 fruit/day
‘Veggies’: indicates that 0 is for not consuming at least 1 veggies/day, 1 is for consuming at least 1 veggies/day
‘HvyAlcoholConsump’: Heavy drinkers (adult men having more than 14 drinks per week and adult women having more than 7 drinks per week) indicates that 0 is for no, 1 is yes
‘AnyHealthcare’: indicates that 0 is for no healthcare coverage, 1 is for having health care coverage
‘NoDocbcCost’: indicates that 0 is for affording a doctor appointment cause of cost within the last year, 1 is for not being able to afford a doctor appointment cause of cost within the last year
‘GenHlth’: personal rating indicates general health scale: scale 1-5 (1 = excellent 2 = very good 3 = good 4 = fair 5 = poor)
‘MentHlth’: indicates days thinking about mental health
‘PhysHlth’: indicates days thinking about physical health
‘DiffWalk’: indicates that 0 is for no difficulty walking down stairs, 1 is for difficulty walking down stairs
‘Sex’: indicates that 0 is for female, 1 is for male
‘Age’: indicates age in years
‘Education’: indicates Education level (EDUCA see codebook) scale 1-6 (1 = Never attended school or only kindergarten; 2 = Grades 1 through 8)
‘Income’: indicates Income scale (INCOME2 see codebook) scale 1-8 (1 = less than 10,000; 5 = less than 35,000; 8 = $75,000 or more)
